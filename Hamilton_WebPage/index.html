Welcome to The Hamilton Project! Hamilton: An American Musical, is a show that follows the life of the United States first Secretary of the Treasury, Alexander Hamilton. It became a worldwide sensation and has since won numerous awards, including 11 Tony Awards, a Grammy, and a Pulitzer prize. Our aim with this project is to analyze Lin-Manuel Miranda's intricate lyrics in order to gain a better understandning of the history, the characters, and the story itself. For more information about the code behind our project, please visit our GitHub repository.
